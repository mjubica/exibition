About Us

We are 5th-grade students at A.C.T. and as part of our exhibition, we are looking at our passions and the SDGs. We want to make a change by connecting our passions to the SDG goal we choose. Our passion is Visual Art but we have different types of Visual Art as our passion. Marin's is lego building Arber’s is photography Aron's is digital animation and Seidy’s is industrial designing. We are gonna use this passion of ours to help change the world. Gender Equality is a very big issue in some countries and it's an issue in Albania too, this is why we choose this SDG. We are linking our passion for Visual Art to Gender Equality by doing Art Activism. Art Activism has many different types of Visual Art, so this is how we are linking our passion to SDG’s Gender Equality.
